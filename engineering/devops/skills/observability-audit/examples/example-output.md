## Executive Summary

Structured logging is in place for `api` and `worker` via `pino` but `webhook` still uses `console.log`. Metrics exist only for `api` via an un-maintained `prom-client` integration; the scrape config points at a disused staging Prometheus. No distributed tracing anywhere. Alerts exist in Alertmanager but three of five fire on cause-based thresholds (CPU > 80%) with no runbook link — two of those have fired 47 times in the last 30 days without a human responding, indicating alert fatigue. No Grafana dashboards in the repo; dashboards live in Grafana UI and have not been exported.

**Biggest gaps:**
1. No distributed tracing anywhere — diagnosing cross-service issues takes hours.
2. Three paging alerts are cause-based and noisy; team ignores them.
3. No correlation ID plumbing — can't link a log line to the request that produced it.
